Hello! I am a current student majoring in Visual Communication and Design. I have a focus in User-Experience design, but I can also code, illustrate, and I have dabbled in immersive experience & mixed reality softwares.
Chat with me about:

Art

Whether we're talking about Renaissance art, Surrealism artists such as Salvador Dali (my favorite), roaming a local art museum, or making our own form of art, I think the craft of this subject is absolutely astonishing, and I could talk about it for days!

Coffee

Okay, this one is not a very unique characteristic, but if someone is suggesting coffee, you best beleive I am going. I get a bit more excited than a normal person should when a new coffee shop opens around town. Plus, what an aesthetically pleasing area to get homework done!

Fashion

Whether it be obsessing over the newest trends, keeping up with name brands, or observing the unique styles each individual has when doing the simple task of dressing themselves up. Fashion to me is another form on non-verbal communication, and I am always listening.

Hiking For a View

I will admit it, I am not a hardcore hiker, but if the hike has a breathtaking view at the end of it, I am all for it. There is something about the views that help me clear my mind, allows me to ground myself, and escape for a little while.

UX

Bear with me, I'm going to geek out a little bit... UX is just so captivating! As a UX designer you get to pick at peoples brains and observe why and how they complete certain tasks. With that, you have the ability to justify your reasoning behind certain design choices with factual evidence. I also love that UX is a perfect blend of science, and creativity for me.
