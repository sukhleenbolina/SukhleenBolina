    • Bio

    My name is Sukhleen (Sue-Clean) Bolina, and my line of works resembles that of a magician... if done correctly. Not the type of magician that pulls rabbits out of a hat, or the ones that say "Abracadabra", but instead, the type that purposefully guides a viewers from one matter to another with ease. The viewer may not even know it's happening. Eastern Washington Universities Visual Communication and Design program has allowed me to do just that. By 2020, I plan on having a Bachelors of Design in VCD, a UX Certificate, and a minor in Communications. My future plans insist of applying to master programs, and eventually becoming a professor teaching design.
